About the Owner!

Hi! I'm Jade - and this is my dog Tobi! I am a 25 year old Software Developer and an artist from Canada! I am the solo developer and owner of Colourful!

It has always been my dream to create a wonderful, easy and safe space for artists, and the art community to enjoy colouring alike. That is exactly what Colourful is! Colourful blends fun, cute and relaxing colouring pages, while making the complex task of digital art, more simple. Colourful was created to be a welcoming space for artists and colorists to unwind and create. Digital coloring can sometimes be confusing, so we made it easier! Cute pages, easy tools, and a relaxing experience that makes art feel accessible to everyone.
